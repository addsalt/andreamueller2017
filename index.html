A bit about Andrea

Andrea’s approach to jewellery is inspired by nature. She works to simplify natural objects and shapes by pairing them down to their most basic elements. Andrea designs and creates her jewellery in Ottawa at her downtown studio space at The Rectory Art House.

How I Work

The Process

  • Step 1

    Nice to meet you

    Let’s get acquainted. Before getting started with any design, it’s important that we meet so I can hear about what you are looking for. The initial appointment is all about sharing ideas, wishes, and expectations.

  • Step 2

    Sketch and Quote

    After our initial meeting, I will create some loose sketches for you to look at and put together quotes for the designs we discussed.

  • Step 3

    Design Approval

    At this point, it works well if we meet again in person. We can iron out any changes that are needed and confirm all the design details before going into the final execution.

  • Step 4

    Create & Reveal

    I begin the process of making the jewelllery. It takes approximately 4-6 weeks to craft a piece. As soon as it is ready, I will be in touch to co-ordinate a pick-up. Exciting!

I strive to create jewellery that has fundamental meaning and symbolism to my customers; that tells a story and connects with them in a way that is bound to stay with them for a lifetime.
